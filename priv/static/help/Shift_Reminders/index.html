Can't remember to track your shifts? Set Shift Reminders!

To use shift reminders, save your schedule by selecting time slots when you're planning to work. You can choose different times on different days of the week.

Once your schedule is saved, click the buttons at the bottom of the screen to choose which reminders you want to receive. You'll get a reminder 10 minutes before your shift is scheduled to start and 10 minutes after it ends.

You must have push notifications enabled to receive Shift Reminders.

If you're taking a break in the middle of your work day, or want to get a reminder to start a new shift, make sure you leave a blank square between filled in slots.

Your schedule is only used for sending shift reminders and will not change your work time on the dashboard.
