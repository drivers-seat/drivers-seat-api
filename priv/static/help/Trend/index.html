Trend
Notice patterns in your total earnings over time.

Trend helps you notice patterns in your earnings over time, from all your connected apps combined.

⚡Figure out which days are best to work, notice trends over time, and see the relationship between your earnings an expenses on different days, weeks, and months.⚡

Here are some stats you will see in Trend:

  • Gross Pay: Total income (including pay, tips, and bonuses).

  • Net Pay: Total pay minus estimated mileage costs.

  • Hourly Pay: How much you earned per hour, calculated for both Gross and Net Pay. Hourly pay is calculated using work time we receive from you apps, plus time you tracked from logging shifts. When you track your shifts, hourly pay will be more accurate because it will include your total work time, and not just time spent on jobs.

  • Expenses: Total expenses you entered manually for the selected time period.
