Dashboard Stats
Learn about stats you can see from your Dashboard (aka Home)

Dashboard Stats give you a quick summary of your earnings and expenses from all your connected apps combined.

⚡Make sure you're hitting your goals and see the relationship between your earnings an expenses on different days, weeks, and months.⚡

From the Home page or Dashboard, you can use the date selector to look at data by Day, Week, or Month. All stats will reflect the time period you selected.

Dashboard Stats

  • Gross Pay: Total income (including pay, tips, and bonuses).

  • Net Pay: Total pay minus estimated mileage expenses. Estimated mileage expenses are calculated using the 2022 IRS mileage rate of 62.5 cents/mile. The best way to make sure your mileage is accurate is to track your shifts, since we don't always get mileage data from your apps. When we do get mileage data from apps, it's usually just mileage from trips, not mileage while driving between jobs.

  • Hourly Pay: How much you earned per hour, calculated for both Gross and Net Pay. Hourly pay is calculated using work time we receive from you apps, plus time you tracked from logging shifts. When you track your shifts, hourly pay will be more accurate because it will include your total work time, and not just time spent on jobs.

  • Expenses: Total expenses you entered manually for the selected time period.
