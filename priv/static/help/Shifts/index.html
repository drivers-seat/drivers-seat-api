Shifts
Learn how to track shifts, why it matters, and how to find the Shift Log.

Get more accurate insights on your work, based on Total Work Time.

When you track your shifts, Driver's Seat will calculate your hourly pay based on Total Work Time. This lets you keep track of time that might not get counted otherwise, like time spent waiting between jobs, traveling to pick up a food order or passenger, and time spent driving back to your work zone.

If you don't track shifts, you might see some time data on the dashboard but it will be incomplete. The time data we get from your gig apps usually just includes time spent on jobs. Track your shifts to make sure all your work time is accounted for!

Unlock more insights when you track your shifts!

  • See Paid Time. Shifts track total time working and allow you to see Paid Time, how much time you spend working versus actually getting paid.

  • Track mileage. This will help you understand how far you've traveling while working, and can be used to report mileage expenses on your taxes.

Why can't I start a shift?

If you see this notification, it means you need to adjust your location settings so you can use the shift tracking feature.

Go to your phone's location settings, and make sure location access is set to Always for Driver's Seat.

Once you set location access to Always, the notification will go away and you will be able to track your shifts!

Shift Log

When you track a shift, it will appear in your Shift Log. The Shift Log shows when you started working, when you stopped working, and how long your shift was.
