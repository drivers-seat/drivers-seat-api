Driver's Seat Co-op Membership

Driver's Seat is different from other driver apps because we're a co-op, owned by rideshare and delivery workers like you.

What is a co-op?

A co-op, or cooperative, is a kind of business that is owned and run by its members for their benefit. Driver's Seat is a user-owned co-op. This means users can get a share of the profits we generate and have a say in deciding the future of Driver's Seat. Our mission is to help gig workers have more control at work, so it only makes sense to make sure gig workers are at the center!

How do I become a member?

As a user, you can become eligible for membership by:

  • Using the app to track shifts on at least 10 days

  • Sharing your feedback by filling our surveys and participating in user interviews

  • Participating in beta testing

When you become eligible for membership, we will send you an invitation to join!

Benefits of membership

  • Shape Driver's Seat development priorities and strategic direction. Control your tech instead of your tech controlling you!

  • Be part of a community. Get connected to other Driver's Seat users.

  • Share in our success. When we become profitable, Driver's Seat members will receive a portion of our profits as dividends in exchange for using the app and being an active member of the co-op. Before that, members will be eligible for gift cards when they participate in co-op building activities like filling our surveys and testing the app, and monthly raffles where you can win Driver's Seat swag and other prizes.
