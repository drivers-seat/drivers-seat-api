How to use Insights
Optimize your schedule with Average Hourly Pay for apps in your area!

Powered by data from rideshare and delivery wokers in your area, this free tool takes the guesswork out of picking the best schedule for you!

See average hourly pay for each gig platform, each hour of the week, with plenty of options for focusing on what matters to you!

Insights Dashboard

From the dashboard, you can see average hourly pay for each gig app you selected. Your settings will appear at the top of the page.

About the data

  • Updated with fresh data a few times a week.

  • Based on averages from the last 3 months, with recent data weighted more heavily so you can notice shifting trends in your area.

  • Estimated mileage expenses are calculated using real mileage data we receive from gig apps. Note that mileage is only included for engaged time (time spent with a passenger or delivery order in your car), so your actual mileage expenses will be higher.

Settings

From the Best Times to Work dashboard, click the Settings icon (⚙️).

Don't see your metro area listed?

If you don't see your metro area listed as an option, it means we don't have enough users in your area to calculate average hourly pay. Here are some ways you can help:

Invite a friend!

You can help bring this tool to workers to your area by recruiting more Driver's Seat users! At the bottom of the Best Times settings page, there is an option to send a friend an invitation to sign up for Driver's Seat.

Make sure your accounts are connected

Another way you can help is by making sure all your gig accounts are connected. From the Main Menu ≡, click Profile, then Services to check your connected accounts. You might need to reconnect your accounts occasionally.
