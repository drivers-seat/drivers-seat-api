Driver's Seat Community Principles
Co-op members are responsible for upholding these community norms.

  • We work to improve the lives of gig workers. We run Driver's Seat for the benefit of gig workers and only collaborate with entities that support our worker-first mission.

  • We support ALL gig workers. We do not take actions that discriminate against or diminish the needs of workers across race, gender, sexuality, ability, income, or other differences. We may face different issues on the job but we are united in our support for each other.

  • We treat each other with dignity and respect. As we work to protect ourselves and each other from disrespect and harassment on the job, we avoid creating harmful dynamics in our interactions with each other.

  • We work together as a community to prevent, interrupt, and heal from harm. We educate ourselves about issues that affect each other to build empathy and understanding. Members are encouraged to request support from staff or other members for hosting workshops for educational or community building purposes. Members are also encouraged to request support from staff or other members to help resolve conflicts and facilitate repair.

  • Members may be removed if found in violation of Driver's Seat Community Principles.
