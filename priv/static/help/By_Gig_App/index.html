By Gig App
See which apps you're earning the most on lately.

By Gig App allows you to compare your work on different apps, so you can see which apps you're earning the most on lately.

⚡Figure out which apps are best to work on on different days. ⚡

  • Gross Pay: Total income (including pay, tips, and bonuses).

  • Work Time: Time spent working in hours. For example if you see 3:01, this means 3 hours and 1 minute. This includes time we receive from your apps (which is usually just time on jobs), plus time from tracking your shifts.

  • Unpaid Time: Time you spent working that you did not get paid for. To see Unpaid Time, make sure you track your shifts.

  • Hourly Pay: How much you earned per hour, based on Gross Pay. On the Platform view, Hourly pay is calculated using work time we receive from your apps.
