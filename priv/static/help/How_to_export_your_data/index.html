How to export your data

Getting ready for tax season? Want to analyze your jobs, earnings, and mileage in a giant spreadsheet without creating it yourself?

Our new data export feature has you covered!

Here's how it works:

1) Select a time period on the earnings dashboard

For example, to see earnings and mileage for all of last year, select Year, then 2022.

Then, click the Download button.

2) Select your export options

Select Include Unpaid Driving Time to include mileage you tracked manually on Driver's Seat. If this option is unchecked, you will only see mileage data from your apps.

Summarize By Gig Platform will show you a breakdown of your earnings and mileage by app.

Summarize by Time Period allows you to see your total earnings and mileage for each Quarter, Month, Day, or Week. If this option is left unchecked, you will see a detailed breakdown of all of your jobs.

3) Check your email!

When your data is ready, you will get an email with a link to download your data in CSV format.

Open the CSV with a spreadsheet app like Microsoft Excel, Google Sheets, or Libre Office Calc.

Read the Data Export Dictionary to understand what each column represents.
