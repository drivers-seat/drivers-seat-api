Drivers Seat: An app for gig workers, owned by gig workers.
Gig workers use Driver's Seat to make more money in less time...and benefit from being owners of the app too!

Gig workers benefit from both our app and our co-op model.

Here's how it works:

Use our app to take control of your work.

Improve your earning strategy so you can make more money in less time.

As a gig worker you know the secret to making money is maximizing your earnings while reducing time and miles. But that's harder than it sounds! When you track your earnings, expenses, and mileage with Driver's Seat, we give you insights that the gig companies won't.

Use Driver's seat to answer questions like:

  • What days and times give me the most money for the least amount of miles on my vehicle?

  • How much time do I spend working compared to actually getting paid?

  • Which gig apps are really best for me once all my time and expenses are accounted for?

Keep accurate, independent records of your earnings, expenses, and mileage.

Make sure you're being paid everything you're owed and track expenses and mileage for your taxes.

Make gig work better, for everyone

City governments and advocacy organizations are looking for ways to make gig work better for everyone. To do that, they need to understand what you think, what you need, and what your works looks like.

That's where we come in! To provide free data insights to gig workers like you, we pool and sell aggregated, anonymized data to institutions that support our worker-first mission. Our data partners use Driver's Seat's aggregated, anonymized data for studies on wages, transportation planning, and other things that make gig work better for you and the communities you serve. It's your data, it should be used in ways that benefit you!

You're in charge!

Driver's Seat Cooperative is owned by rideshare and delivery workers like you.

Being a co-op means you get to share our success.

When we sell insights from your collective data, we return profits to users who have joined Driver's Seat as co-op members.

You decide.

Do you have an idea for a new app feature? Is there a specific issue you want us to collect data on or work on through our partnerships? As a member, you can shape the future of Driver's Seat.

Being a gig-worker owned co-op means we are committed to running our business in a way that always works for your benefit.
