Research Gigs
How to enroll and unenroll in a research study.

We partner with city governments, advocacy organizations, and other institutions that are friendly to our worker-first mission on studies that help inform things like transportation planning and wage policy.

If you ever participate in a research gig, we will send you a code that you can use to enroll in the app.

Navigation

From the Menu, click Research Gigs.

Enrolling in a study

To enroll in a study, navigate to Research Gigs and click Yes.

Next, enter the code provided to you and click Submit.

If you enter a valid code, you will see the name of the research study. Click on the drop-down icon to read a description of the study and supplemental terms of use.

Read the description. It will contain important information about your participation and what to expect. It will also include any supplemental terms of service. You will need to accept these to participate in the study.

When you're done reading the description and terms, click the checkbox to accept and enroll in the study.

Unenrolling in a research gig

If you want to unenroll in a research gig because you signed up for the wrong one, no longer want to participate, or the gig ended, click Unenroll below the research gig description.
